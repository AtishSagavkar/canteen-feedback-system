How It Works

Use the feedback form to rate food items and canteen experience. Your feedback is saved securely in Firebase and shown to admins on demand.

  1. Choose Student or Teacher role.
  2. Rate canteen areas and each food item using stars.
  3. Add a comment and submit.
  4. Admin can open dashboard to see total counts and averages.
